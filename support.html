Frequently asked questions

Does Guard Your Pay connect to my bank account?
No. Guard Your Pay does not connect to your bank, and there's no login or account required. You enter your pay and outgoings manually, and everything is calculated on your device.
Where is my data stored?
All your data stays on your phone. Nothing is sent to a server, and we don't collect, store, or have access to any of your financial information.
Is this an official MOD or British Army app?
No. Guard Your Pay is an independent app built for British Army personnel. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or produced by the Ministry of Defence or the British Army.
The figures for my allowance or pension look wrong — what should I do?
Figures shown in the app are estimates intended to help you understand your pay and entitlements — they are not a substitute for your unit HR, JPA, or official MOD guidance. If something looks off, please email us so we can look into it, and always confirm anything important with your chain of command or HR staff.
Why does the app ask which rank or accommodation type I have?
This information is used only to calculate your estimated daily safe-to-spend figure and allowance entitlements. It's stored locally on your device and is never sent anywhere.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email support@guardyourpay.app with a description of what happened and, if possible, a screenshot. This helps us fix issues faster.
